# AP Luxury Content Guide

Use this guide when replacing placeholder content with final AP's Thread Salon content.

## Brand Voice

Write with a calm, premium, confident voice. Keep sentences clear and welcoming. Avoid discount-heavy language unless the page is specifically for promotions.

## Primary SEO Phrases

Use these phrases naturally across page titles, headings, image alt text, and body copy:

- eyebrow threading Rockwall TX
- threading salon Rockwall
- facial threading Rockwall
- waxing Rockwall TX
- brow shaping Rockwall
- beauty salon Rockwall TX

## Homepage

Use the homepage for the strongest booking message. The hero should clearly mention brows, threading, and Rockwall. Keep the first button focused on booking.

## Services Page

Verify every price before launch. Use plus pricing only if the final amount can vary. Keep service names simple and easy for customers to understand.

## Gallery

Replace placeholder gradients with real salon images. Recommended image types:

- Clean salon interior
- Threading station
- Brow shaping result
- Facial room detail
- Product or towel detail
- Exterior storefront

Use compressed WebP images when possible.

## Reviews

Connect a Google Reviews plugin on the Testimonials page. Keep selected written testimonials short and believable.

## Promotions

Use promotions for first-visit offers, referral offers, gift cards, and monthly brow maintenance packages. Add expiration dates when needed.

## Contact and Booking

Confirm phone, address, hours, booking URL, Instagram URL, and map information before launch. Test click-to-call on a real phone.

## Final Tone Test

Each page should make the salon feel clean, skilled, warm, and trustworthy.
