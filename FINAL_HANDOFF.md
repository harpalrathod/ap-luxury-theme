## Shortcodes

Use these in WordPress content areas:

- `[ap_booking_button]`
- `[ap_phone]`
- `[ap_address]`
- `[ap_offer]`

## Before Going Live

1. Upload the real AP's Thread Salon logo.
2. Add the real phone number, address, hours, booking link, Instagram link, and map details in the Customizer.
3. Replace placeholder image blocks with real salon photos.
4. Connect booking software.
5. Connect reviews, Instagram, and contact form plugins.
6. Review all prices, promotions, privacy text, and terms text.
7. Complete `RELEASE_CHECKLIST.md`.

## Recommended Plugins

- Rank Math SEO
- WPForms
- Smash Balloon Instagram Feed
- Google Reviews plugin
- LiteSpeed Cache or WP Rocket
- Imagify or ShortPixel
- WooCommerce only if selling gift cards, packages, or products
